Kind food

Kind food is the idea that people can eat foods kinder to the animal, environment, or each other.

Kinder Foods

The kind food movement stands proudly, chewing quietly.

Fit from table to body

Food and other products -even meat- should be considered on ethical grounds to one’s self, community, and even the sentient lives of the animals used in creation of any particular product.

Fit from body to mind

This does not necessitate vegetarian or vegan consumption, but for some may, however 98% of food eaters are omnivorous and it isn’t expected to change overnight.
